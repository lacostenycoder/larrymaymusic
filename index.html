Larry May

Singer / Songwriter / Producer

From numerous airings on network and cable TV shows All My Children, One Life to Live, The Young and Restless, NCIS, Parenthood, Mike and Molly, Melrose Place, 90210, America’s Prom Queen, Caroline in the City, Party of Five, Summerland, DeGrassi: The Next Generation, Teen Cribs, Sunday’s Best, True Life, Laguna Beach, Harper’s Island, Co-Ed Confidential, Dogs in the City and Hollywood Heights to a finalist in international songwriting contests, Larry May, a native New Yorker, has been writing, recording and performing in the U.S and abroad for a number of years. A multi-talented singer/songwriter and producer with guitar chops to match, his thought-provoking lyrics and memorable melodies make his music top-notch.
